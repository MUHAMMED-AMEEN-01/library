About Our Library

Discover our mission, values, and the team behind your library

Our Story

Library is located inside Grace Valley College of Arts & Science at Wadirrahma, Maravattam, Chengottoor P O., Malappuram Dist., Kerala. College was established in 2004 as a Self-Financing Degree College under the management of IKHVANI TRUST.

Our Mission

We believe in free access to necessary information for all college members.

"To empower our generation through knowledge, inspire lifelong reading, and connect people through shared reading experiences in a welcoming environment."

Our Values

  • Accessibility for all college members
  • Commitment to literacy and education
  • Preservation of local history and culture
  • Innovation in library services
  • Environmental sustainability
